Welcome to the newly structured Digital Brain Blog! This section will be used to share updates, engineering thoughts, and progress on building a personal knowledge base.

<!-- more -->

## Why a Blog?

While the **Docs** section acts as an evergreen digital garden — a place for structured knowledge, guides, and reference material — the **Blog** is designed for time-bound thoughts. 

Here, you'll find:
- 🚀 **Release Notes:** Updates on the Digital Brain infrastructure.
- 💡 **Engineering Logs:** Deep dives into technical challenges (like Eleventy configuration, CSS architecture, and D3 graph views).
- 📚 **Learning Journeys:** Step-by-step chronicles of new topics being explored.

## Structure

We write posts in standard Markdown. The static site generator automatically parses the frontmatter to sort by date and categorizes them correctly on the blog index page.

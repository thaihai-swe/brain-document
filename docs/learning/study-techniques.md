# Study Techniques

Effective techniques for learning and retaining technical knowledge.

## Active Learning Methods

### Feynman Technique
1. Choose a concept
2. Explain it in simple terms
3. Identify gaps in understanding
4. Review and simplify further

### Spaced Repetition
- Review material at increasing intervals
- Use tools like Anki for flashcards
- Focus on weak areas
- Regular practice over cramming

### Mind Mapping
- Visual representation of concepts
- Shows relationships between ideas
- Great for system design

## Coding Practice

### Deliberate Practice
- Focus on specific skills
- Work slightly beyond comfort zone
- Get immediate feedback
- Repeat with variation

### Project-Based Learning
1. Start with a clear goal
2. Break into small tasks
3. Build incrementally
4. Refactor and improve
5. Document learnings

## Time Management

### Pomodoro Technique
- 25 minutes focused work
- 5 minutes break
- Repeat 4 times
- Longer break (15-30 min)

### Time Blocking
- Dedicate specific hours to learning
- Minimize distractions
- Track progress
- Adjust as needed

## Retention Tips

!!! note "Key to Retention"
    Teaching others is one of the best ways to solidify your own understanding.

- **Teach what you learn**: Blog, mentor, or explain to peers
- **Build real projects**: Apply knowledge immediately
- **Review regularly**: Revisit concepts periodically
- **Connect to existing knowledge**: Link new concepts to what you know
- **Practice retrieval**: Test yourself without looking at notes
